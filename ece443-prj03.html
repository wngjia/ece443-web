I. Objective

A lot of software and hardware products that one may use everyday contain solutions to protect certain aspects of security. In this project, you will study one topic based on published papers, reports, or news items to understand the technical details of its security solution and possible issues.

For your convenience, here is a list of papers, reports, and news items, each corresponding to a possible topic. Please choose one for your project.

  • Why Johnny Can't Encrypt: A Usability Evaluation of PGP 5.0
  • XcodeGhost

  • The Sorry State of TLS Security in Enterprise Interception Appliances
  • The Return of Coppersmith's Attack: Practical Factorization of Widely Used RSA Moduli
  • LastPass says employee's home computer was hacked and corporate vault taken and LastPass Security Incident Update and Recommended Actions
  • The 23andMe Bankruptcy Part 1 and Part 2
  • Anker's Eufy lied to us about the security of its security cameras
  • Popular app to spy on its users and detailed backdoor report
II. Deliverables

Submit the following to Blackboard for this project.

  1. A project report of no less than 1000 words. In particular, please address the items below (you may need to perform additional research online).
    • The threat and the security policy, i.e. what should be protected?
    • Technical details of the security solution or issues with it.
    • Is the security solution effective? Why or why not? If yes, will there be any new threats? If no, how to improve the security solution?
